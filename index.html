How to play Beat the Deck

Guess if the next card will be higher or lower than the current card on your chosen stack. Use all remaining cards to BEAT THE DECK!

Ace is high, 2 is low. Same card value is not allowed.

Burned stacks are removed from the game. Burn all 9 and you're out!

Jokers are wildcards that can be higher or lower than any other card.

Use the Check Stacks power-up to see the previously placed cards in active stacks.

Use the Sneak Peak power-up to see the next card in the active stacks.
