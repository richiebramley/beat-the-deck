About Beat the Deck

Beat the Deck is a free online card game that challenges your strategic thinking and luck. Based on the classic "Higher or Lower" concept, this game adds a unique twist with multiple stacks and strategic decision-making.

Use all remaining cards in the deck by correctly guessing whether the next card will be higher or lower than the current card on your chosen stack.
