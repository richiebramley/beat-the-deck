How to play Beat the Deck

Objective

Guess if the next card will be higher or lower than the current card on your chosen stack. Use all remaining cards to BEAT THE DECK!

Card Values

Ace is high, 2 is low. Same card value is not allowed - you must choose higher or lower.

Jokers

Jokers are wildcards that can be higher or lower than any other card.

Burned Stacks

Burned stacks are removed from the game. Burn all 9 stacks and you're out!

Power-ups

Check Stacks: See previously placed cards in active stacks
Sneak Peak: See the next card in active stacks

Streak System

Track your consecutive successful placements! Build the longest streak possible to improve your score.
